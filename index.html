Final Year Project

Subject Area: Geotechnical Engineering

Title: The would-be importance of Kaolin Clay Plasticity

The main aim of the project was to understand the role and importance placed on plasticity index (a material property specific to soil) in geotechnical modelling, with precedence to physical modelling and a focus on progressive failure. To achieve this, a rigorous literature survey was carried out by sifting through studies to locate most relevant to the project. MATLAB and Python was used to analyse and present d data in a comprehensive manner in the main project report and final presentation. Aneisha identified and reported limitations in the project, conclusions, and suggested recommendations for future work based on her extensive literature-survey and data analysis.
